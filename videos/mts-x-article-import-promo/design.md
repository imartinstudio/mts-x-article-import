# X Article Import Promo Design

## Palette
- Background: #0B0F17
- Surface: #121927
- Elevated: #182235
- Foreground: #F5F8FF
- Muted: #AAB6C8
- Soft: #DCE8F7
- Accent: #1D9BF0
- Accent Deep: #1267A8
- Line: #2B3B54

## Typography
- Display: Inter, system sans-serif
- Body: Inter, system sans-serif
- Mono: JetBrains Mono, SFMono-Regular, Menlo, monospace
- Headline weights: 800-900
- Body weights: 400-600

## Shape And Depth
- Radius: 24px, 32px, 44px
- Depth: layered glass cards, soft blue glows, crisp 2px borders
- Spacing: generous video scale, 56-120px padding

## Motion
- Medium-high SaaS launch energy
- Primary transition: directional block wipe
- Accent transition: blue scan bar and soft overexposure
- No jump cuts; every scene has entrance choreography

## Avoid
- Purple/cyan generic gradients
- Tiny UI text as primary message
- Flat black backgrounds without texture
